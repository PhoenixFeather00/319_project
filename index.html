    Zach and Rand's Favorite Games & Genres

    When looking into video games, it's hard to know exactly what you’re going to buy without trying it yourself. There are online reviews and some stores have user reviews, but most of the time those are either overly complicated to read coming from journalism sites with too many ads or the reviews on the store page are nonsensical and don’t give you the information you need. Thus, a website that has those short and concise reviews without any of the difficulty while adding our experience to give genuine reviews about games is what we aim to achieve.
